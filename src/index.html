Welcome

Life is full of challenges for all of us. Some we are able to resolve alone and others require the courage to find some professional help. I can provide a safe and discrete environment to talk about your worries and help make sense of them.

People turn to counselling for different reasons. Symptoms of anxiety and depression are common reasons for reaching out, or maybe you simply feel a loss of direction in your life. Therapy is about working together towards unravelling and resolving these difficulties. More broadly it is about personal growth, freedom and moving towards a more fulfilling existence.

The services I offer include adolescent counselling, generally over the age of 16, with sessions for parents if appropriate, and relationship counselling, be it familial, romantic or otherwise.

My practice is based in the heart of Edgbaston (Birmingham) , easily accessible via public transport and offering ample private parking. I also have appointments available in Tarporley (Cheshire) , during the week. Evening appointments are available at both practices.

Through developing a new understanding of your life and increased self-awareness, you can learn to be kinder to yourself and others, have more control, take more responsibility and have the confidence to live the life you want to lead without fear of judgement or criticism.

For counselling and psychotherapy enquiries please contact me anytime. If I am unable to take your call, I will get back to you within 24 hours.
